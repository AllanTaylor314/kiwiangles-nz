At Kiwi Angles, we allow you to bring a piece of New Zealand home. We have a selection of photographs from around the country particularly the Canterbury region. Each photograph has a letter found in iconic spots across our beautiful country. Spell out your name or any words you desire. Try out some words here, or view our full selection of letters in the gallery.

We are a year 13 Commerce team participating in the Young Enterprise Scheme (YES). Our mission for this year is to inspire creativity as you view New Zealand through a new lens.
